Dear Professor ,

Thank you very much for being interested in volunteering in our event.
The schedule can be found at http://deltahacks.io/

The Idea Generation Group happens on Saturday from 11:00AM - 12:00PM for an hour. It is a chance for anyone who has an idea that can be achieved using technology, yet do not have the technical skill to do it. They will have 1-5 minutes to pitch about their idea to attract programmers.

Talk/Workshop will be an extended version of Idea Generation, where you have 15-60 minutes to talk about a problem you are passionate about. Talks should take place mostly from Saturday afternoon til night, so people can get familiar with the problem and then start work on them.

Mentors do not have to be here all the time, but we advise them to work with the students until they are clear of the problem they are working on. They also do not need to be tecnical. When groups of students are interested in and work on a problem you talked about, since they may have very limited knowledge about the problem, you can guide them throughout the hackathon and make sure they are on the right track of solving the problem.

Judging will take place on 12-4PM on February 15th, 2015.

The following are sign-up links:

Mentor signup

Talk/Workshop (during the hackathon)
http://bit.ly/workshopSignup

Idea generation signup (they pitch their problem at the beginning of the hackathon)
http://bit.ly/idea-gen

If you have any questions, please feel free to email me.

Regards,
