Introducing Vshore

Vshore is more than an application that can be used to buy, trade or share items. It is a platform to share ideas, feelings and stories with friends and family. It is also a centralized ecommerce site where a community of people who have items they no longer need or desire can economically offer them to benefit others. Vshore, simply stated, can enrich the lives of others by providing people they know with things they need.
