Introducing Vshore

Vshore is more than an application that can be used to buy, trade or share items. It is a platform to share ideas, feelings and stories with friends and family. It is also a centralized ecommerce site where a community of people who have items they no longer need or desire can economically offer them to benefit others. Vshore, simply stated, can enrich the lives of others by providing people they know with things they need.

See what your friends are selling

Using Vshore will always keep you in touch with friends and make it easy to view new items posted for trading or buying. Vshore is a natural platform for sharing thoughts, unique products and making extra dollars for things you no longer want or need. One click and you have entered a new space where you can share thoughts, ideas and be part of a virtual world full of your friends belongings, unwanted furnishings, clothes, equipment and everything imaginable.

  • Common

  • Share
    Share items and products that others need that you do not.
  • Offer
    Ask questions about what it is for sale from those you know and buy or trade something special.
